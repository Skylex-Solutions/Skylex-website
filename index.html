How it works

From request to delivery in three steps.

01

Submit

Employees submit a document through the Skylex portal. The system extracts text, counts words, and calculates an SLA deadline automatically.

02

AI translates

Lexii, our proprietary AI translator, and DeepL translate every segment in parallel, applying your glossary and translation memory for consistent terminology. Images with text are translated too.

03

Validator reviews & delivers

An assigned linguistic expert reviews, edits, and approves each segment. The final document is generated and delivered — in your original format, layout preserved.

Features

Everything your team needs. Nothing it doesn't.

AI-powered translation

Lexii, our proprietary AI translator, works alongside DeepL in parallel. Automatic glossary application, translation memory reuse, and image-in-document translation included.

Enterprise SSO

Works with any OIDC-compatible identity provider — Microsoft Entra ID, Okta, Auth0, Google Workspace, Ping, Keycloak. Role mapping from your directory.

All major formats

DOCX, PPTX, XLSX, XLIFF, PDF, plain text — layout-preserving translation with lossless re-export. Your document comes back looking exactly as submitted.

Validator workspace

A structured CAT-tool-style interface for linguistic reviewers. Segment-by-segment editing, DeepL suggestions, TM matches, and one-click approval.

Multi-tenant architecture

Each client gets their own subdomain, Key Vault, and isolated configuration. Onboard a new enterprise client with zero code changes or redeployment.

Audit & compliance

Every translation edit, approval, and delivery is logged with a timestamp and user ID. Full audit trail for regulated industries.
